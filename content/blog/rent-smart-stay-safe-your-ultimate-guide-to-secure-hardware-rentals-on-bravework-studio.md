### **For Renters: Protecting Your Project & Peace of Mind**

When you're renting hardware, you're not just renting a machine; you're often engaging in a valuable exchange. Here's how to ensure it’s secure from start to finish:

1.  **Verify, Verify, Verify:** Before you even consider a rental, ensure your Bravework Studio account is fully verified. This simple step helps us maintain a trusted community and adds a layer of accountability for all users.
2.  **Choose Verified Vendors:** Always prioritize renting from vendors with a "Verified" badge on Bravework Studio. These users have met our stringent criteria, indicating a higher level of trustworthiness and reliability.
3.  **The Ultimate Safety Net: Share Your Plans:** For physical hardware exchanges, one of the most proactive steps you can take is to inform a trusted family member or friend about your rental plans. Share the vendor's profile information, the exact location of the exchange, and the estimated time. This simple yet powerful step ensures someone knows your whereabouts and plans, providing an invaluable layer of personal security and peace of mind.
4.  **Guard Your Digital Footprint:** Never, ever, store your personal files or documents permanently on rented hardware.
    *   **Cloud is Your Friend:** Utilize cloud storage services (like Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, etc.) for all your project files. Upload what you need, work on it, and download it back to your cloud or personal storage.
    *   **External Drives Are Essential:** For larger files or projects, use your own external hard drive. This keeps your data physically separate and secure.
    *   **Cloud Security Best Practices:** If you access cloud services on rented hardware, **always remember to log out of your accounts** before returning the equipment. And crucial for any online account: **always enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)**. It's your strongest defense against unauthorized access.
5.  **Confirm Satisfaction, Release Funds:** At Bravework Studio, your peace of mind extends to payment. After every session, you'll be prompted to **confirm your satisfaction and readiness to release funds** before the auto-release occurs. This empowers you with direct control, ensuring you're completely happy with the service and the hardware's condition before the transaction is finalized. It's an integral part of our commitment to your safety and satisfaction.

### **For Owners: Securing Your Assets & Data**

Renting out your valuable hardware can be a fantastic income stream, but it requires smart practices to protect your own digital environment and assets:

1.  **Dedicated User Accounts:** The golden rule for owners is to create a separate, standard user account on your PC specifically for third-party usage. Never give renters access to your administrator account or your personal user profile. This isolates your files, settings, and personal data from anything the renter does.
2.  **Sandbox for the Super-Secure:** For advanced users seeking the highest level of isolation, consider setting up a sandbox environment. A sandbox is an isolated virtual space where the renter can operate without affecting your main operating system. It's like a temporary, disposable workspace. [Link to Set Up Sandbox Post]
