In this guide, we will walk you through the essential steps of creating a simple 3D character.

### 1. The Box Modeling Technique

Start with a simple cube. Use the edit mode (Tab) to extrude and scale the faces to form the torso and head.

### 2. Adding Symmetry

Use the 'Mirror Modifier' to ensure your character is perfectly symmetrical. This saves you half the work!

### 3. Sculpting Details

Switch to Sculpt Mode to add smooth curves and organic details to your character.

### 4. Final Export

Once satisfied, export your model as an .OBJ or .FBX file ready for animation.
